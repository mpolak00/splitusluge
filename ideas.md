  **Design Movement**: Mediterranean Minimalism with Brutalist Accents

  - **Arch Shapes**: Rounded top corners on cards, mimicking Roman arches.
  - **Bold Typography**: Large, condensed headings for service categories.

  **Interaction Philosophy**:
  - Hover effects that "lift" cards slightly, like boats on water.
  - Fast transitions, emphasizing efficiency (Split locals value speed).

  **Animation**:
  - Gentle fade-ins for content.
  - "Wave" effect on buttons when clicked.

  **Typography System**:
  - **Headings**: 'Barlow Condensed' (Bold/ExtraBold) - Modern, strong, impactful.
